📚 FAQ & Agent Training
Add frequently asked questions and answers about your company, products, pricing, or services. The AI agent will refer to these during calls to answer customer queries accurately. Write each Q&A on its own line or in any format you prefer.
Suggested format:
Q: What is the price of your IoT sensor?
A: Our basic IoT sensor starts at ₹4,500 per unit with bulk discounts available.

Q: Do you offer free installation?
A: Yes, free installation is included for orders above 10 units.

You can also write in Hindi or use bullet points — the agent understands any format.
